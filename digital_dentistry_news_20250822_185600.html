    In "4 Steps You Can Start Now To Increase Case Acceptance," Scott J. Manning suggests a four-step approach for dental practices to increase case acceptance, profitability, and build long-term relationships with patients. The first step is setting the stage by using pre-exam materials such as videos, welcome packets, and documentation that communicate the comprehensive approach of whole-health dentistry. This helps change a patient's mindset from a simple checkup to a more significant conversation about overall health and well-being. The second step involves expanding vision and self-diagnosis during the clinical experience by asking patients to rate their oral health on a scale, and then discussing how treatment can help them achieve a higher ranking. Finally, create lifelong patients post-visit by scheduling follow-up appointments for untreated issues, maintaining a structured follow-up process, appreciating and surprising patients with small touches, and turning happy patients into advocates for the practice through referrals, reviews, and testimonials. Caregivers upload oral health concerns via the app, which includes patient details, medical history, and photos, all within a secure digital environment.
